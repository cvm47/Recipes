Colombian-Coke

Description:

The Colombian-Coke aka THE COLOMBIAN CULT. A refreshing white powder that's definitely sugar. Straight from the lush green hills of Colombia. Paying its homage to The Man, The Myth "EL-CHAPO".

Ingredients:

  • Cocoa leaves
  • Gasoline
  • Ammonia
  • Sulfuric acid
  • Baking soda
  • Hydrochloric acid

Steps:

  1. Harvesting Coca Leaves - Farmers pick the mature leaves of the Erythroxylum coca plant and then sun dried.
  2. Maceration & Extraction - The leaves are soaked and mashed with chemial like: Gasoline, Baking soda and water.
  3. Separation & precipitation - Sulfuric acid is added to form wet paste called pasta basica(basic paste). the paste is filtered and dried
  4. Purification - The paste is dissolved with ammonia to purify it into a crystalline base. This base is often transported and sold to Cartel run labs.
  5. Final form- Base is dissolved in solvents and processed with hrydrochloric acid, forming cocaine-hydrochloride-the fine white sugar.
